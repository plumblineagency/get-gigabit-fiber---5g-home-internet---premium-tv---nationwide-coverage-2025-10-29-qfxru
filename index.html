These speeds are ideal for 4K streaming, large file downloads, video conferencing, and online gaming. Unlike cable or DSL, fiber provides symmetric speeds, meaning your upload speeds match your download speeds. We guarantee 99.9% uptime, so you can count on consistent performance 24/7. 5G home internet uses next-generation wireless technology to deliver broadband speeds without requiring fiber installation. It's perfect for areas where fiber isn't yet available. While speeds may vary based on location and network conditions, 5G typically delivers 100-500 Mbps, which is excellent for most households. The advantage is quick installation—no digging or running cables through your home. You get the benefits of wireless connectivity with speeds that rival traditional broadband. Both fiber and 5G are excellent options; we recommend fiber if available in your area, but 5G is a fantastic alternative where fiber isn't accessible.
